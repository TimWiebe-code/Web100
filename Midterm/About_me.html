Hometown

I grew up in and still live in Leamington, Ontraio. It is a small town that does not have much going for it hoenstly, but it is still my home. It is well known for it's tomato greenhouses. One of the best locations to visit in Leamington would be Seacliff park. It is on Lake Erie and has lots of stuff to do. There is a beach, small but still nice, a playground for kids to play as well as a small splash pad. Seacliff also has new basketball courts the have been recently build as well as a very nice walking path around the park.
