Favourite Band: AJR

AJR is a band of three brothers, Adam Met, Jack Met, Ryan Met. AJR uses very obscure instraments in their songs that aren't often heard. their music can be very upbeat and happy, or very slow and sad. AJR is a band that can make music for any mood. They started back in 2005 and are still performing and making music today.
